About

Nat is a born-and-raised resident of the Northern Rivers region, who has provided IT support for businesses for 10 years. Versed in various languages as part of his work, Nat is moderately proficient in C#, PowerShell, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A spread of skills picked up over his years of work solving bespoke technival issues and writing custom solutions to meet customer needs. Setting his sights to pursue his lifelong passion of becoming a programmer proper, Nat is currently undertaking an intensive 6-month coding bootcamp to hone his techique and open the door to web app development, aiming to become skilled enough to create more robust tools that will not only improve the efficiency of his coworkers and himself, but to also enable him to delve deeper into debugging or creating web apps for the ever-changing needs of his clients.

When he isn't working or coding, Nat is often relaxing with various video games, delving into Virtual Reality, and learning to do 3D modelling.
